FREQUENTLY ASKED

Booking a consultation is easy through our online system. Click the "Booking" button on any page, select your preferred artist, and choose an available consultation slot. Consultations are $50, which is credited toward your tattoo if you proceed. During the consultation, you'll discuss your ideas, placement, sizing, and receive a custom quote.
Our artists typically book 2-3 months in advance, with some availability for smaller pieces sooner. Large-scale projects may require booking 4-6 months ahead. We recommend booking your consultation as soon as you're ready to commit to your tattoo journey, as this secures your place in the queue.
Our artists work at premium rates reflecting their expertise and the quality of work. Hourly rates range from $250-$400 depending on the artist and complexity. Most pieces start at $500 minimum. Large-scale projects are quoted as complete packages. We require a 30% deposit to secure your appointment, with payment plans available for extensive work.
We operate primarily by appointment to ensure each client receives dedicated time and attention. However, we occasionally have same-day availability for smaller pieces. Follow our Instagram @berserk_tattoos for walk-in announcements, or call the studio to check current availability.
We understand plans can change. We require 48 hours notice for rescheduling to avoid forfeiting your deposit. For large-scale appointments, we prefer 72 hours notice. Same-day cancellations result in deposit forfeiture and may affect future booking privileges. We appreciate your understanding as this policy ensures fairness for all clients.
