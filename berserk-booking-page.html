BOOKING POLICIES

DEPOSIT & PAYMENT

  • 30% deposit required to secure appointment
  • Deposits are non-refundable but transferable
  • Final payment due on day of appointment
  • Payment plans available for large projects
  • Accepted: Cash, Card, Bank Transfer

CANCELLATION POLICY

  • 48 hours notice required for rescheduling
  • Less than 48 hours forfeits deposit
  • No-shows forfeit deposit and booking privileges
  • Emergencies handled case-by-case
  • Maximum 2 reschedules per booking

HEALTH & SAFETY

  • Valid ID required (18+ only)
  • No tattoos if pregnant or nursing
  • Disclose all medical conditions
  • No alcohol/drugs 24 hours before
  • Arrive well-rested and fed

AFTERCARE INCLUDED

  • Premium aftercare kit provided
  • Detailed care instructions
  • Follow-up check-in after 2 weeks
  • Touch-ups included if needed
  • 24/7 aftercare support line
