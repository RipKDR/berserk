FIRST TATTOO GUIDE

  • Welcome to the beginning of your tattoo journey! We specialize in making first-time experiences memorable:

    • Research styles: Browse our artists' portfolios to find what resonates
    • Consider placement: Think about visibility and pain tolerance
    • Start reasonable: Your first tattoo doesn't have to be small, but consider your comfort
    • Book a consultation: We'll guide you through every step

    Our artists are experienced in working with first-timers and will ensure you feel comfortable and informed throughout the process.

  • Pain is subjective, but most clients describe it as manageable discomfort rather than severe pain:

    • Less painful areas: Arms, thighs, calves, shoulders
    • More sensitive areas: Ribs, feet, hands, neck
    • Duration matters: Longer sessions can be more challenging

    We work at your pace and can take breaks whenever needed. Many clients find the experience meditative once they settle in.

    Pain Management Tips: Get plenty of rest, eat a good meal beforehand, stay hydrated, and remember to breathe!

  • Proper preparation ensures the best experience and results:

    • Night before: Get 8+ hours of sleep, avoid alcohol
    • Day of: Eat a substantial meal, shower, wear comfortable clothing
    • Bring: ID, water, snacks, headphones/entertainment
    • Avoid: Alcohol, drugs, aspirin, excessive caffeine

    Arrive 15 minutes early to complete paperwork and settle in.

AFTERCARE

  • Proper aftercare is crucial for healing and preserving your tattoo's quality:

    • First 3 hours: Keep the bandage on
    • Day 1-3: Wash gently with antibacterial soap, pat dry, apply thin layer of provided aftercare balm
    • Day 4-14: Continue washing 2-3 times daily, moisturize regularly
    • Week 2-4: Let scabs fall off naturally, continue moisturizing

    Premium Aftercare Kit Included

    Every tattoo comes with our curated aftercare products and detailed instructions.

  • Protect your investment by avoiding these during the healing process:

    • Swimming pools, hot tubs, ocean water (3-4 weeks)
    • Direct sunlight and tanning beds (4 weeks minimum)
    • Picking or scratching at scabs
    • Tight clothing over the tattoo
    • Excessive sweating/gym for first 48 hours
    • Petroleum-based products

    Following these guidelines ensures optimal healing and color retention.

  • We stand behind our work with a comprehensive touch-up policy:

    • Free touch-ups within 6 months if you follow aftercare instructions
    • Must provide photos of healing process if requested
    • Touch-ups cover any areas that didn't heal properly
    • Does not cover damage from sun exposure or neglect

    Most tattoos heal perfectly with proper care and don't require touch-ups.

STUDIO POLICIES

  • We understand plans change, but please respect our artists' time:

    • 48+ hours notice: Deposit transfers to new appointment
    • Less than 48 hours: Deposit is forfeited
    • No-shows: Deposit forfeited and booking privileges may be revoked
    • Emergencies: Handled case-by-case with documentation
    Tip: Add your appointment to your calendar with reminders to avoid accidental no-shows.

  • We maintain strict age policies for legal and safety reasons:

    • Must be 18 years or older
    • Valid government-issued photo ID required
    • No exceptions for parental consent
    • International passports accepted

    Please bring your ID to every appointment, including consultations.

  • We welcome support, but maintain a focused environment:

    • One guest allowed for moral support
    • Guest must remain in designated waiting area
    • No children under 18 in the studio
    • Service animals welcome with documentation

    Our studio space is designed for artist concentration and client comfort.

  • Your safety is paramount. Please disclose if you have:

    • Diabetes, hemophilia, or blood disorders
    • Heart conditions or pacemakers
    • Epilepsy or seizure disorders
    • Pregnancy or nursing
    • Skin conditions (eczema, psoriasis)
    • Allergies to latex, metals, or inks
    • Medications that affect blood clotting

    We'll work with you to ensure a safe tattoo experience or recommend waiting if necessary.
